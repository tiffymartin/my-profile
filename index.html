Rive Droite Vespa Club 🛵

Swisstour | By Tiffany Martin

Swisstour is an event organised by Rive Droite Vespa Club every summer since 2018. The dates and the itinerary of the tour vary every year according to the availabilities and ideas of the members. The goal of the tour is to discover new landscapes, villages, cultures and to have an adventure in Switzerland or in the neighbouring countries. In 2022, we decided to explore new horizons and travel to Sardinia. We started our journey in Geneva (as always) and headed south to Turin. On our second day we made our way to Genova to embark on a night ferry. We arrived in Olbia early morning the next day. We stayed on the island for a week, it was a beautiful trip and we can't wait for the next one! Next year we will take part in the World Vespa Days in Interlaken. See you there!
